Our Success Story

Bhookha Panda started as a small tiffin service, driven by passion and a love for good food. Today, we have evolved into a successful cloud kitchen, delivering a variety of delicious meals right to your doorstep. Our journey is all about innovation, teamwork, and a commitment to providing quality food to thousands of satisfied customers.

Prashnat – The Visionary Leader

Prashnat, the visionary behind Bhookha Panda, has guided us through this exciting transformation. His leadership has been crucial in taking Bhookha Panda from a humble tiffin service to a thriving cloud kitchen that serves customers with speed and quality.

Vanshikha – Graphic Designer

Vanshikha’s creativity helped us redefine our brand as we transitioned into a cloud kitchen. Her eye-catching designs ensure that our brand stands out, representing the fresh and flavorful food we deliver every day.

Shailja – Marketing Expert

Shailja’s market expertise has been vital in scaling Bhookha Panda as a cloud kitchen. Her sharp marketing strategies have helped us reach more customers than ever before, making sure we are always top-of-mind when it comes to great food delivery.

Deepak – Web Designer & Technical Expert

Deepak has played a key role in making our cloud kitchen accessible to everyone online. His technical skills have allowed us to streamline our operations, making ordering from Bhookha Panda fast, easy, and reliable.
